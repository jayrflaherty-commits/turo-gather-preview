🔥 "Turnover Autopilot"

Hosts spend 2.4 hrs/week coordinating vehicle handoffs — finding cleaning slots, managing check-in photos, and chasing late returns. 68% want an integrated turnover scheduling tool. Highest-priority unmet need.

⚡ "Smart Pricing Confidence"

52% of hosts manually adjust prices weekly but only 23% trust Turo's dynamic pricing suggestions. Hosts want transparent "why" explanations alongside price recommendations. Key retention lever for power hosts.

📊 "Earnings Intelligence"

Hosts managing 3+ vehicles want fleet-level P&L dashboards — depreciation tracking, per-vehicle ROI, tax prep exports. Currently cobbling together spreadsheets. Premium feature opportunity.
